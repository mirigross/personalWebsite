Muddy Buddies:

Ingredients:

  • 9 cups of rice chex
  • 1 cup of semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar

Directions:

  1. in a large bowl measure the cereal and set aside
  2. microwave chocolate chips and peanut butter, uncovered until it can be stirred smooth. pour mixture into cereal and stir until evenly covered
  3. add powdered sugar

    4. cool muddy buddies before eating!
